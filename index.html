Current project:

100new Experiences, 27 Jun–04 Oct 2015

Day 26: Sebastian

27 February, 2015 in Brussels

26 years old

From Belgium, currently living in London

Speaks English and French

Describe the meeting circumstance

My friend introduced me to Sebastian. As we were sitting in a cafe waiting for him, the meeting was preplanned in a way so I wanted to try an experiment. I brainstormed different introductory first liners because often times we introduce ourselves with a hello, my name is, how are you and etc other common phrases. When Sebastian arrived, I was inspired by his jacket and introduced myself as "Hi, I'm Teodora. Nice jacket, can I have it?” He nervously laughed and said “Erm thanks, you can try it on I guess.” He proceeded to sit down, take off his jacket, place it on a chair and change the topic.

What I learned

First liners are important. After explaining to him my project, Sebastian admitted thinking that I was bizarre at first. Then he warmed up and was very nice, as noted in the photo when he even let me borrow the jacket for the photo.
